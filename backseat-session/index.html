backseat sessions

2011 - 2016

Personal side-project featuring bands, acoustic guitars and moving vehicles.

These live-music session videos are intimate little portraits of musicians (literally) on the road. As this was a personal pet-project I basically covered all roles; from contacting bands and arranging the shoot to the filming, post-production and online publication.

tools: DSLR, Zoom, Lapel Mics, Adobe Suite and gallons of gasoline

crew: Mark van de Korput and friends
