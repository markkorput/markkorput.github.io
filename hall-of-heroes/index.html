Hall of Heroes

July 2016

www.afgefackt.com | www.overhetij.nl

A video-installation by Afgefackt for the 2016 edition of the Over Het IJ theatre festival in Amsterdam. The installation featured three synchronised video projectors, each connected to a RaspberryPI. Playback on all devices was controlled using a single MIDI controller, connected to one of the Raspberry PIs.

crew: Afgefackt (Mirjam van Dijk and Élénie Wagner), Two Feathers (Davey Bakker and Hayo de Kruijf), Mark van de Korput, Huig van den Hoofdakker, Florian Knapp.

my role: installation design and installation controls

tools: RaspberryPI, python, OSC, MIDI
