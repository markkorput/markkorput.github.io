Art Wuzhen | Children of the Light

The Children of the Light had two installations at 2019â€™s edition of Art Wuzhen in China. One of the installations was Light Ceremony Space which was shown for the first time at ADE 2017 in Amsterdam and consists of a single bright LED light and two DMX-controlled electric motors; one winch for vertical movement and one rotor for circular movements.

For this installation I created the original software which I adapted to be controllable by the crew of the Art Wuzhen exhibition.

my role: software development, on-site installation
